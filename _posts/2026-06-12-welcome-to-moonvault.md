Every game master has a place where the campaign really lives. Sometimes it is a notebook, sometimes a folder full of documents, and sometimes it is twenty browser tabs opened five minutes before the session.

**Moonvault is the place I wanted behind my own GM screen:** a local-first tabletop RPG campaign manager where worlds, campaigns, characters, locations, encounters, and session notes stay connected without leaving my computer.

## Prep the world, then run the table

Moonvault stores your vault as portable Markdown files, so your campaign notes remain readable and yours. Inside the app, you can:

- Build worlds and organize multiple campaigns.
- Connect NPCs, locations, factions, lore, and sessions with wikilinks and backlinks.
- Prepare encounters, roll initiative, track HP, conditions, rounds, and turns.
- Create random tables, roll dice, and keep a useful roll history.
- Manage fantasy calendars, maps, parties, monsters, and player characters.
- Use system-aware tools for D&D 5e, Shadowdark, OSE, Tormenta20, or system-agnostic games.

There is search when I forget the name of the tavern, pinned pages when the party refuses to leave it, and a focused session mode for the moment the players choose the one road I did not prepare.

## Your campaign stays yours

Moonvault does not require an account or a custom cloud. A vault is a normal folder that can live on your machine, in Dropbox or iCloud Drive, or wherever you already keep important campaign files.

That is the heart of Moonvault: **fast game-master tools on top of durable, portable RPG notes**.

The vault is open. Welcome to Moonvault.
